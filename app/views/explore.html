Rotavio Labs is also actively developing various simulation models that are useful for helicopter design and analysis.
RLDyn

RLDyn is a structural dynamic analysis software for rotor blades. The main source of vibration for a helicopter is its Main Rotor. It helps understand the blade properties and design it for minimum vibrations. To reduce structural dynamic vibrations - an in depth understanding of vibrations emanating from main rotor and fuselage response to these vibratory loads is necessary. RLDyn will be helpful in evaluating mode shapes and natural frequencies of a composite rotor blade with advanced blade geometry.

RLCar

RLCar is a comprehensive aeroelastic and flight dynamics simulation program that will be useful to solve helicopters general maneuver problem. It predicts rotorcraft loads by solving highly nonlinear aeroelastic responses. RLCar is useful in calculating rotorcraft performance, trim, blade motion & air-loading, structural loads, vibration, noise, aeroelastic stability and flight dynamics.

RLCon (under development)

RLCon is a Helicopter Control systems simulation software. The software uses an in-house developed model of the helicopter as plant. It is used to observe the closed loop position hold performance by gain tuning in software. This helps reduce the time required for gain tuning in actual flight of the helicopter.
