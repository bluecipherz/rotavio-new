ROTAVIO
Rotavio Labs Private Limited is a company whose genesis occurred in the Helicopter Lab of IIT Kanpur in the year 2011. It is mentored by esteemed faculty of the Aerospace Department of IIT Kanpur. Rotavio Labs specializes in the area of end-to-end research and development of fully autonomous Helicopters. The team has comprehensive understanding of the intricacies involved in design, development and manufacturing of rotorcrafts, their avionics and control systems. We are based out of Hyderabad.
